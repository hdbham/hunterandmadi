Location

Our special day will take place at YMCA Mill Hollow (7480 South Mill Hollow Road, Kamas, UT) a stunning and secluded spot nestled in the Uinta Mountains. We love its rustic beauty, but want guests to be prepared for its quirks! The site is remote, featuring unpaved roads, limited service, and abundant fresh mountain air; well worth the drive! This isnt your typical wedding venue; think more pine trees and campfires than ballrooms and chandeliers. Please review the location before RSVPing, as travel may be more difficult or take more time than you're used to. Overnight guests - we recommend packing layers, sturdy shoes, and anything youll need for a mountain getaway. We will provide a packing suggestion list if you plan to stay the night! Thank you for joining us on this little adventure. We cant wait to celebrate with you! Please review the options below if you plan to attend the whole weekend.
